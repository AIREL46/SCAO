Invention project

The realisation of our culinary concept "Quiet Cook" results in the development of a technology named Smart System of Cooking (SSC). It automates cooking monitoring, including low-temperature cooking. SSC is made up of three connected items: a smartphone, an electronic-handle, and an electrionic contact breaker (see schema below). Those three items communicate together via Virtual Wifi Network. The smartphone outputs parameters to the electronic-handle. This handle performs temperature measurments with the pan's lid and, after having analysed those measurments, it outputs an appropriate energy quantity to the electronic contact breaker: an automatic temperature regulation is enabled.
