Overall introduction

A3CP aims to revisit traditional culinary concept and practices. Main objectives are nutritional conservation of food, energy gain, and human well-being.

It is materialised through an invention project which consists in several prototypes of Culinary Systems of Cooking CSC. Prototypes' developments are made in France, in Pays de la Loire, in Presqu’île de Guérande.

Within A3CP, a team is in charge of those developments; the members are volunteers. Their activities are diversified: cooking, design, mechanics, electronics, wiring, electrics, computing, web design. If you want to join our team, there are missions to be completed on Benevolt. The means of our team are located in the headquarters of A3CP, in St Molf. They consist mainly in a documentation, 3 prototyping workshops and a supply.

The intellectual property rights of the invention project (CSC) are patent-free and use Creative Commons licences related to Creative Commons France.
